The _orthogonal projection_ of some vector \\(v \in \mathbb{R}^n\\) onto some subspace \\(W\\) of \\(\mathbb{R}^n\\) is the vector \\(v\_W \in W\\) such that \\(v\_W\\) is closest to \\(v\\) out of all vectors in \\(W\\). In other words,

\\[
v\_W = \arg \min\_{u \in W} ||u - v||.
\\]
